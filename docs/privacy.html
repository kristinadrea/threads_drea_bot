Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Drea Threads Bot is a private automation tool used to crosspost selected content from Telegram channels managed by the app owner to the owner's Threads account.

Information the app processes

The app may process Telegram channel posts, captions, message identifiers, and media metadata from channels explicitly configured by the app owner. If image crossposting is enabled, the app may temporarily download images from Telegram and upload them to configured public storage so they can be published to Threads.

Information the app stores

The app stores minimal technical state needed to prevent duplicate publishing, such as identifiers of processed Telegram messages and weekly posting state. The app does not store passwords or payment information.

How information is used

Information is used only to publish selected content to the owner's Threads account and to keep the automation reliable. The app does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for advertising or profiling.

Third-party services

The app uses Telegram, Threads by Meta, and, if configured, Cloudflare R2 or another storage provider for image hosting. These services process data according to their own terms and privacy policies.

Data retention

Technical state is kept only as long as needed for the automation to function. The owner may delete the local state files at any time.
